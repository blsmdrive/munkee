Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my first task in munkee?

+

Tap the "+" button to create a new task. Give it a name, choose a category and priority level, add a due date if needed, and watch a monkey appear on your back! The monkey's face will reflect the priority you chose.

What are the swipe gestures and how do I use them?

+

Swipe left on any task to mark it complete - watch the monkey jump off your back! Swipe right to delete a task permanently. These are standard iOS gestures, so they'll feel natural and familiar.

How do custom categories work?

+

Create categories that match your life - Work, Personal, Health, Creative projects, anything! Each category can have its own custom name and color. Organize your monkeys by the areas of your life that matter most.

What do the different monkey faces mean?

+

Each priority level has its own monkey personality! High priority tasks show stressed or worried monkeys, medium priority tasks have neutral expressions, and low priority tasks display calm, relaxed monkeys. It's a fun visual way to see what needs attention.

Is my task data stored privately?

+

Absolutely! All your tasks, categories, and progress stay completely private on your device. munkee doesn't have servers, doesn't require accounts, and can't see your data. Your monkeys are yours alone.

Can I add notes or details to my tasks?

+

Yes! Each task can include detailed notes, reminders, or additional context. Tap on any task to edit it and add as much detail as you need. Perfect for project steps, shopping lists, or important reminders.

How do due dates and overdue tasks work?

+

Set due dates for time-sensitive tasks. munkee automatically detects overdue tasks and highlights them so you can prioritize accordingly. The monkeys for overdue tasks might look a bit more urgent!

Does munkee work on iPad as well as iPhone?

+

Yes! munkee is designed as a universal iOS app that works beautifully on both iPhone and iPad. The interface adapts to your screen size while maintaining the same delightful monkey experience.

What about Dark Mode and accessibility features?

+

munkee fully supports iOS Dark Mode and automatically adapts to your system preferences. We also include VoiceOver support, Dynamic Type for text scaling, and haptic feedback for a fully accessible experience.

Can I track my productivity and completion progress?

+

Yes! munkee tracks your completed tasks with timestamps, so you can see your progress over time. There's something deeply satisfying about watching your productivity improve as more monkeys jump off your back.
